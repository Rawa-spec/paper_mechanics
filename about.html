Paper mechanics provides an hour-long session with enriched arts and crafts materials in our classrooms. Milestone projects are provided to our members to keep our students motivated to learn more advanced origami. Our classes have books on origami, accessible to our students and free origami beginner books are provided to take away when you join your first session. Our students are given a personal origami toolkit.
